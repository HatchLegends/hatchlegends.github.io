Game Overview

Hatch Legends

Hatch Legends is a premium creature collection game built around hatching eggs, discovering rare creatures, and building a growing collection over time.

Core Loop

Hatch eggs, reveal creatures, collect discoveries, and progress through a satisfying creature-focused progression system.

Egg Rarities

Eggs are designed with clear rarity identity, clean hatch stages, and strong collectible readability across common, rare, epic, and legendary tiers.

Creature Identity

The game centers around cute, premium, collectible fantasy creatures with polished silhouettes, readable forms, and satisfying reveal moments.

Codex Progression

Players grow their collection over time, track discoveries, and fill out their creature catalog as they continue hatching and progressing.

Discord First

Hatch Legends is launching on Discord first for testing and iteration, with a longer-term direction toward iOS.

Long-Term Vision

A premium-feeling creature collecting experience with clean visuals, strong progression, and a memorable collectible identity.
