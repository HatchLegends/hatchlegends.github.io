Game Overview

Hatch Legends

Hatch. Collect. Evolve. Battle.

A creature collection RPG built around hatching, progression, and discovery.

Hunt for rare eggs, incubate them, battle in raids, overcome trials, and climb the leaderboards.

Egg Tiers

Eggs come in four tiers: Common, Rare, Epic, and Legendary. Each tier increases in rarity and visual intensity.

Essence System

Creatures are aligned with distinct essences that define their strengths, roles, and interactions in battle. Build synergistic teams and adapt your strategy around essence matchups.

Codex

Collect and track every creature. Complete your codex and uncover the rarest forms.

Progression

Level up your creatures and unlock stronger evolutions as your collection grows.

Active Play

Hunt, hatch, and optimize your strategy. Active players progress faster.

Idle Rewards

Earn passive rewards over time, providing steady growth alongside active play.

Future Plans

Arena battles with auto-strategy, deeper trial systems, and advanced incubators will expand progression and long-term depth.
