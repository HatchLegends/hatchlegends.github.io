Game Overview

Hatch Legends

Hatch. Collect. Evolve. Battle.

A creature collection RPG built around discovery, progression, and mastery.

Hunt & Hatch

Search for rare eggs and awaken the creatures within.

From simple forms to powerful, rare beings — every hatch is a chance at something greater.

Egg Tiers

Eggs exist in four tiers: Common, Rare, Epic, and Legendary.

Higher tiers are harder to find and hold creatures of greater power, rarity, and visual intensity.

Grow & Evolve

Strengthen your creatures through progression and evolution.

The more you invest, the more they reveal their true potential.

Master Combat

Every creature is shaped by its Essence.

Creatures align with distinct Essences that define their strengths, roles, and interactions in battle.

Build synergistic teams, counter opponents, and adapt your strategy to dominate.

Complete the Codex

Discover and collect every creature.

Track your progress and uncover the rarest forms hidden within the world.

Progress Your Way
