Essences

Essence is the living signature of manifested life. It shapes temperament, affinity, abilities, and the way a creature grows, fights, and resonates with the world.

The Five Known Essences

Verdant favors growth and resilience. Ember drives aggression and explosive force. Tide adapts through flow and control. Volt emphasizes speed and precision. Void bends toward absence, instability, and distortion.

Verdant

Growth, resilience, and adaptation.

Ember

Heat, aggression, and explosive power.

Tide

Flow, control, and shifting force.

Volt

Speed, precision, and sudden bursts of energy.

Void

Absence, distortion, and unstable power that defies natural form.
