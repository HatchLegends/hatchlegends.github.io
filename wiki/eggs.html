Eggs & Manifestation

Genesis Eggs are the first stable vessels of living potential. Through incubation and manifestation, possibility becomes life.

Genesis Eggs

Genesis Eggs hold multiple possible forms until stabilized. They are not just containers, but living thresholds between potential and identity.

Incubation

Incubation is the careful preparation of a Genesis Egg for safe awakening through time, attunement, and guidance.

Manifestation

Manifestation is the moment a Genesis Egg becomes a single, real creature. Stable guidance lowers the risk of collapse or distortion.

Starfalls

Many eggs descend into Virelya through starfalls, introducing new life and new creature lineages into the world.
