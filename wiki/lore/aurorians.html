The Aurorians

Aurorians stand at the edge of perfected manifestation, carrying the clearest known echo of Aurelion.

Aurorians — The Living Truths

Aurorians

Aurorians are the rarest and most complete expressions of manifested life. Their forms are exceptionally stable and their resonance unusually pure.

They are proof that manifestation can approach perfection even within a world threatened by imbalance and unmaking.

Aurorian Primals

Primals

Primals are a rare subset of Aurorians. Each represents the highest known expression of a core Essence embodied in near-perfect form.

  • Astrael — Void
  • Pyrael — Ember
  • Verdael — Verdant
  • Tidael — Tide
  • Voltael — Volt
  • Solrael — a fragmented sixth reference tied to an unknown or lost Essence
