Section 2

Primordial Forces

These concepts describe how creation condenses, descends, awakens, and reaches different degrees of completion.

Genesis Eggs — The Origin of All Forms

Genesis Eggs

Genesis Eggs are the first stable vessels of living potential. Within them, life exists in perfect possibility, not yet shaped but ready to emerge.

The quality of a Genesis Egg depends on the strength, clarity, and stability of the Aether within it.

Manifestation — The Awakening

Manifestation

Manifestation is the moment a Genesis Egg stabilizes and becomes a single, real creature.

A successful manifestation results in a complete living being. An unstable manifestation may weaken the creature, alter its form, or fail entirely.

Starfalls — The Descent

Starfalls

Starfalls are the descent of Genesis Eggs into the world. They introduce new life, new lineages, and new possibilities into Virelya.

Irregular starfalls are rare but increasing, and many believe that change is linked to strain in the Balance.

Aurelion — The Infinite Origin

Aurelion

Aurelion is believed to be the highest source of pure creation. It is described as a first perfect origin through which Aether entered ordered existence.

The rarest and most complete manifestations are said to carry an echo of Aurelion within them.

Rarity — Degrees of Manifestation

Rarity

Rarity reflects the degree to which a life-form has manifested its inner Aether. It is not an imposed ranking, but a natural outcome of manifestation.

At the highest levels, manifestation approaches completeness rather than survival, producing beings whose existence feels closer to pure creation itself.
