World & Lore

This archive follows the same structure as your lore notes and breaks the setting into clean, readable section pages. Start with the foundations of existence, then move outward into manifestation, ascendants, the lost age, and what lies beyond known reality.

Aetherion, Aether, the Balance, and the Null. This is the cosmological base of the setting.

Section 2

Primordial Forces

Genesis Eggs, manifestation, starfalls, Aurelion, and rarity: the path from possibility to life.

World of Manifestation

Section 4

Section 5

The Lost Age

The Aeon Architects and their disappearance: the vanished masters behind the surviving fragments of knowledge.

Section 6

The Aurorians

Aurorians and Primals: the rarest known expressions of stable, perfected manifestation.

Section 7

Beyond Known Reality

The Unknown beyond Aetherion, where familiar law and stable resonance may no longer apply.
