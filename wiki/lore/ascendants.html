The Ascendants

The Aetheri and their tools sit at the center of safe manifestation, creature growth, and the preservation of Balance.

Aetheri — The Ascendants

Aetheri

Aetheri are beings capable of stabilizing Genesis Eggs and guiding manifestation without breaking the life within.

They do not force life into being. They help it cross the threshold safely and preserve creation as instability increases.

  • Seek out Genesis Eggs
  • Stabilize manifestation
  • Form Aetherlinks
  • Train and evolve creatures
  • Defend against distortion
  • Preserve the Balance

Aetherlink — The Bond of Resonance

Aetherlink

Aetherlink is the living bond formed between an Aetheri and a manifested being through shared Aether alignment.

It is not ownership or obedience. It is mutual recognition, stable partnership, and a path toward deeper growth.

Codex — The Record of Manifestation

Codex

The Codex is a living record of manifested life. Originally developed by the Aeon Architects, it documents Essences, manifestations, and the behavior of Aether across different conditions.

Modern Codices are reconstructions, updated as new creatures and new patterns are discovered.

Incubation

Incubation

Incubation is the disciplined art of preparing a Genesis Egg for safe manifestation through time, energy, and careful attunement.

It is not simply waiting. It is the act of helping possibility become life without breaking what makes that life unique.
