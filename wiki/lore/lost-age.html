Aeon Architects — The First Ascendants

Aeon Architects

The Aeon Architects were the first known masters of guided creation. They built the first true incubation systems and the earliest form of the Codex.

Fragments of their systems still shape how modern Aetheri understand manifestation, even though their mastery has never been fully recovered.

The Disappearance — The Lost Ascendants

The Disappearance

The Aeon Architects did not fall. They vanished. Their structures remain, but their presence is gone and their records end abruptly.

After their disappearance, instability increased. Starfalls became less predictable, manifestation failures became more common, and distortion began to surface across Virelya.
