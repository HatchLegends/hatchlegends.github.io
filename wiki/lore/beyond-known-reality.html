Beyond Known Reality

Not all of existence is mapped, understood, or stable. Some possibilities sit beyond the known structure of Aetherion itself.

The Unknown — Beyond Aetherion

The Unknown

Beyond Aetherion lies what no living age has fully mapped. The oldest records suggest there are regions beyond familiar creation where the laws of manifestation fail and resonance behaves unpredictably.

The Unknown is feared not because it is proven hostile, but because it lies beyond every stable law that makes life possible.

Some believe the Aeon Architects did not disappear, but crossed into it. If true, they may still exist beyond the reach of Aetherion in a place where the Balance no longer applies.
