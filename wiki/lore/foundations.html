Section 1

Foundations of Existence

Aetherion, Aether, the Balance, and the Null define the deepest structure of reality in Hatch Legends.

Aetherion — The Living Universe

Aetherion

Aetherion is not a place. It is a living system: a universe shaped by energy, Balance, and creation itself.

Everything that exists emerges from Aether, forms through Balance, and risks being undone by the Null. This is the foundation of all life, all creatures, and all power within Hatch Legends.

Nothing in Aetherion is truly inert. Worlds grow, energies gather, and life emerges where resonance allows it.

Aether — The Living Energy

Aether

Aether is the living energy from which all form emerges. It is the first substance of creation, flowing through worlds, matter, consciousness, and life itself.

Where Aether gathers, reality becomes possible. Where it stabilizes, existence takes shape.

When Aether manifests into life, it expresses itself through distinct patterns known as Essences.

The Balance — The Law Between Light & Null

The Balance

The Balance is the law that preserves existence. It prevents creation and unmaking from consuming all reality.

Without Balance, creation would become unstable excess. Without Balance, unmaking would spread without limit until nothing remained.

As disruptions increase, the role of the Aetheri becomes critical because they help stabilize a system under strain.

The Null — The Unmaker

The Null

The Null is the force of unmaking. It is not death, ruin, or decay. Those are still forms of existence. The Null is something far more absolute: the removal of being itself.

Where it takes hold, things do not break down. They cease. It does not create, and it does not corrupt in the ordinary sense. It unthreads reality from its foundation, stripping away form, memory, resonance, and continuity.

It often acts indirectly by disrupting Aether flow, destabilizing starfalls, and interfering with manifestation. These events are increasing and are understood as signs that the Balance is weakening.
