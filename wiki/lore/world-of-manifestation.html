Section 3

World of Manifestation

This is the lived world of Hatch Legends: where starfalls arrive, creatures awaken, Essences define identity, and distortions threaten stability.

Virelya — The Convergence World

Virelya

Virelya is the convergence world where starfalls, stable Aether, and manifested life reliably meet in the same place.

It has become the center of egg discovery, incubation, and creature development, but it is also the first place where fractures in the Balance are felt.

Stellaris — The Starfall Herald

Stellaris

Stellaris helps guide Genesis Eggs into Virelya, aligning their descent with the natural flow of Aether.

It does not create starfalls, nor fully control them. Its nature exists somewhere between system and being.

Essence — The Living Signatures

Essence

Essence is the defining signature of manifested life. It shapes temperament, affinity, abilities, and the way a creature interacts with the world.

The five known Essences are Verdant, Ember, Tide, Volt, and Void.

Convergence Arenas — Fields of Resonance

Convergence Arenas

These are controlled spaces where creatures can battle without risking uncontrolled manifestation or collapse.

Combat inside them is not just conflict. It is a method of growth, refinement, and deepening Aetherlink.

Distortions — Fractures of the Null

Distortions

Distortions are regions where the Balance weakens and Aether becomes unstable. Manifestation in these zones often becomes altered, dangerous, or incomplete.

At their worst, distortions can produce powerful unstable entities and spread into surrounding regions if left unchecked.
