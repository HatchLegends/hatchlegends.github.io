<h2 align="center">Core Systems</h2>

### Codex
Collect and track every creature. Complete your codex and uncover the rarest forms.

### Progression
Level up your creatures, strengthen your roster, and unlock stronger evolutions over time.

### Active Play
Hunt, hatch, and optimize your strategy. Active players progress faster.

### Idle Rewards
Earn passive rewards over time, giving your collection steady growth alongside active play.

### Future Plans
Arena battles, deeper trial systems, and advanced incubators will expand progression and long-term depth.
